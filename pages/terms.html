The current page is here to explain the terms of use in the Game Hallows community. You must follow them at whole time.

Be respectful, responsible & don't use bad words

It may sound obvious, but bad words are forbidden. If someone is bothering you just mention a people in the staff and see what happens ;) | Using hard bad words may lead you to be sanctionned with magnitude.

Don't spam, flood or advertise

Spamming is forbidden as it pollutes the text channels. It is not funny to find a little message beyond tenths of useless messages.

Don't lend, sell, steal or leave any account

Lending or selling an account is forbidden by the Discord ToS. Stealing an account may be illegal, I don't know... Leaving accounts on our server will perturbate our server statistics.
